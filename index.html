About

HEJ! My name is TOVE JANSSON.



Born in Helsinki, Finland, I grew up in a family of filled with artists. My mother was a Swedish-speaking Finnish illustrator and my father was a sculptor. I was the eldest of four siblings, and I was the only one who pursued a career in the arts. I was a painter, illustrator, and author, but I am best known for creating the Moomins, a family of white, roundish trolls with large snouts that make them resemble hippopotamuses. The Moomins are the central characters in a series of books and a comic strip that I wrote and illustrated, starting in the late 1940s, and which became popular worldwide.
